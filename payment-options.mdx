## Payment security

All transactions on NinjaHackz are processed securely. Your payment details are encrypted and handled by our payment processor — NinjaHackz does not store your full card number or financial credentials.

## Choosing your payment method

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credit or debit card">
    At checkout, enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV. Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express are all accepted. Your card is charged immediately upon order confirmation.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PayPal">
    Select PayPal at checkout and you'll be redirected to log in to your PayPal account to confirm the payment. You're then returned to NinjaHackz and your order is processed automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Apple Pay or Google Pay">
    If you're on a supported device and browser, Apple Pay or Google Pay will appear as a checkout option. Tap to confirm with your device authentication and the payment completes instantly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## If your payment is declined

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check your card details">
    Make sure the card number, expiry date, CVV, and billing address you entered match exactly what your bank has on file. Even a small mismatch can cause a decline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Verify available funds or credit">
    Ensure your account has sufficient funds or available credit to cover the purchase. Contact your bank if you're unsure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Try a different payment method">
    If one method is declined, try a different card or switch to PayPal, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. Multiple payment options are available at checkout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contact your bank">
    Some banks flag unfamiliar transactions and block them as a precaution. Call the number on the back of your card to authorize the charge, then try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reach out to NinjaHackz support">
    If you've tried the steps above and your payment still won't go through, contact NinjaHackz support for help resolving the issue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Once a payment succeeds, your software download and license key are available immediately in your account dashboard. A confirmation email is also sent within seconds. If your payment failed, no charge is made and no order is created.
</Note>
