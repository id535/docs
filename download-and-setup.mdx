After a successful purchase, NinjaHackz delivers your software instantly — no manual processing and no waiting. You can access your download from two places: your account dashboard (available immediately after payment) and your confirmation email (sent within seconds). Both contain the same download link and license key.

## Accessing your download

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Account dashboard">
    After payment, you are redirected to your account dashboard automatically. Your download link, license key, and account password setup are all available there. This is the most reliable way to access your software at any time.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Confirmation email">
    A confirmation email is sent to your registered address within seconds of a successful payment. It contains a direct download link and your license key.

    <Note>
      The download link in your confirmation email is temporary and expires after 3 days. After that, log in to your account dashboard to access your download.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Download and installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your purchase">
    After payment is confirmed, you are automatically redirected to your account dashboard. Orders are processed 24/7, so access is immediate regardless of when you purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your account dashboard">
    Log in to your NinjaHackz account if you are not redirected automatically. Your dashboard displays your download link, license key, and an option to set your account password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your account password">
    If this is your first purchase, set your account password before continuing. Look for the password setup prompt on your dashboard and follow the instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the software">
    Click the download link on your dashboard. Save the installer file to a location you can easily find, such as your **Downloads** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the installer">
    Open the downloaded installer file and follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation. Accept the license agreement when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your license key">
    When the software launches for the first time, you will be prompted to enter your license key. Copy the key from your account dashboard or confirmation email and paste it into the activation field. See [License Keys](/license-keys) for detailed activation steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Finding your download after the email link expires

If your confirmation email link has expired, you can always retrieve your download from your account dashboard.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How to access your download from the dashboard">
    1. Go to the NinjaHackz website and click **Log In**.
    2. Sign in with your email address and account password.
    3. Navigate to your dashboard — your download link and license key are listed there.
    4. Click the download link to start the download again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I can't log in to my account?">
    Use the **Forgot password** option on the login page to reset your password. If you did not set a password after your initial purchase, check your confirmation email for a setup link or contact NinjaHackz support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I never received a confirmation email?">
    Check your spam or junk folder first. If the email is not there, log in to your account dashboard directly — your download and license key are available there regardless of whether the email was delivered.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Do not share your download link or license key with others. Your license is tied to your account and is intended for your personal use only.
</Warning>
