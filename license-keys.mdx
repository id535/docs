Your license key is a unique code that unlocks your copy of NinjaHackz software and ties it to your account. You receive it the moment your payment is processed — it appears on your account dashboard immediately after purchase and is also included in the confirmation email sent within seconds of a successful payment.

## Where to find your license key

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Account dashboard">
    After payment, you are redirected to your account dashboard. Your license key is displayed there alongside your download link. You can return to the dashboard at any time to retrieve your key — it does not expire.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Confirmation email">
    Your confirmation email contains your license key alongside your download link. The email is sent to your registered address within seconds of a successful payment. Search your inbox for an email from NinjaHackz if you do not see it immediately, and check your spam folder if needed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Copy your license key and store it somewhere safe, such as a password manager or a secure notes app. While you can always retrieve it from your account dashboard, having a backup copy saves time if you need to reinstall or activate on a new device.
</Tip>

## Activating your license key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the software">
    If you have not already installed NinjaHackz, download and run the installer from your account dashboard. See [Download & Setup](/download-and-setup) for the full installation walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch NinjaHackz">
    Open the NinjaHackz application. On first launch, the software displays an activation prompt automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your license key">
    Go to your account dashboard or open your confirmation email. Select and copy the full license key, including any hyphens or special characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the key into the activation field">
    Return to the NinjaHackz activation prompt and paste your license key into the provided field. Make sure there are no extra spaces before or after the key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm activation">
    Click **Activate** or **Submit** to complete the activation. If successful, the software will confirm your license and take you to the main interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting activation issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The key is showing as invalid">
    Double-check that you copied the full license key without any missing characters or extra spaces. Paste the key again directly from your account dashboard rather than retyping it manually. Keys are case-sensitive, so ensure your input matches exactly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The key has already been used">
    Each license key is tied to your account. If you are reinstalling on the same device or setting up a new one, contact NinjaHackz support to have your activation reset.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find my license key">
    Log in to your account dashboard — your license key is always listed there regardless of whether you received the confirmation email. If you cannot log in, use the **Forgot password** option on the login page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The activation prompt is not appearing">
    Try closing and reopening the software. If the prompt still does not appear, look for a **License** or **Activate** option in the application menu or settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Do not share your license key. It is linked to your account and is intended for your personal use only. Sharing it may result in your license being deactivated.
</Warning>
