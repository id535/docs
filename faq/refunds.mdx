# NinjaHackz CS2 Cheats Refunds & Cancellations FAQ

NinjaHackz delivers software instantly after purchase, so it is important that you review all service details carefully before completing your order. The sections below explain the refund policy, your cancellation options, and how to try the software before buying.

<Warning>
  All sales are final. No refunds are available for any purchases or subscriptions, regardless of circumstances.
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the refund policy?">
    NinjaHackz does not offer refunds on any purchases or subscriptions. Because software is delivered instantly and access is granted immediately after payment, all sales are considered final. Please review the service details carefully before purchasing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription?">
    Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees and no minimum commitment period required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    To cancel, log in to your account on the NinjaHackz website and manage your subscription from your account settings. Cancellation is self-service and takes effect according to your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my access when I cancel?">
    When you cancel your subscription, your access continues until the end of your current billing period. After that period ends, your subscription will not renew and access to the software will end. No partial refunds are issued for unused time remaining in a billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a way to try before buying?">
    Yes. A trial option is available for a small fee. This gives you the opportunity to evaluate the software before committing to a full subscription or purchase. Look for the trial option on the NinjaHackz website at checkout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
