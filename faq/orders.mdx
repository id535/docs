# CS2 Cheats Orders & Delivery FAQ: NinjaHackz Instant Access

When you place an order on NinjaHackz, everything is handled automatically — no waiting for a human to process your purchase. The sections below answer the most common questions about payments, delivery, and getting started with your software.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What payment options are accepted?">
    NinjaHackz accepts the following payment methods:

    - PayPal
    - Visa
    - MasterCard
    - Discover
    - American Express
    - Apple Pay
    - Google Pay

    All payments are processed securely at checkout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does delivery take?">
    Delivery is instant. Orders are processed automatically 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. As soon as your payment is confirmed, your software and license key are ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after I pay?">
    After a successful payment, you are redirected to your account dashboard. From there you can:

    - Download your software
    - Access your license key
    - Set up your account password

    You will also receive an email within seconds containing your download link and license key.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where is my download link and license key?">
    Your download link and license key are delivered in two places:

    1. **Your account dashboard** — log in to the NinjaHackz website at any time to access them.
    2. **Your email inbox** — an email is sent to you within seconds of a successful payment.

    If you do not see the email, check your spam or junk folder.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My download link expired — what do I do?">
    The download link sent to your email is temporary and expires after 3 days. After it expires, log in to your account on the NinjaHackz website to download your software at any time. Your account dashboard always has an active link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a trial option?">
    Yes. A trial is available for a small fee. This lets you evaluate the software before committing to a full purchase or subscription. You can find the trial option on the NinjaHackz website at checkout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
