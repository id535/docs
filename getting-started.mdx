# Quick Start: NinjaHackz CS2 Cheats & Mods Setup Guide

This page walks you through everything you need to do after purchasing NinjaHackz — from completing your order to downloading the software and activating your license key so you can start hacking challenges right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit the NinjaHackz website and sign up for an account. You will use this account to manage your subscription, access downloads, and retrieve your license key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Browse the available subscription plans and select the one that fits your needs. If you are new to the platform, consider starting with the trial option before committing to a full subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Proceed through checkout and complete your payment. Orders are processed automatically 24/7, so your purchase is confirmed immediately regardless of the time of day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    After payment, you are redirected to your account dashboard. From here you can download the software, view your license key, and set up your account password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the software">
    On your dashboard, locate the download section and grab the latest version of the NinjaHackz software. Your download link is also sent to your email within seconds of payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your license">
    Copy your license key from the dashboard or your confirmation email and enter it when prompted during software setup. Your license is tied to your account and activates instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The download link sent to your email is temporary and expires after 3 days. If you need to download the software again after that window, log in to your account dashboard to access a permanent download link.
</Note>

<Tip>
  If you are new to NinjaHackz, take advantage of the trial option available for a small fee. It lets you explore the platform before purchasing a full subscription, so you can make sure it is the right fit before you commit.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  NinjaHackz does not offer refunds. Please review the plan details, system requirements, and any trial option carefully before completing your purchase.
</Warning>
