## What is NinjaHackz?

NinjaHackz provides hacking challenge software delivered digitally the moment you pay. There's no physical shipment — your download link and license key are available immediately in your account and are also emailed to you within seconds of a successful payment.

You can choose from subscription plans or a trial option if you want to test before committing. Your account dashboard is your central hub for everything: downloading software, managing your license, and handling billing.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Browse available plans on the NinjaHackz website. A trial option is available if you want to evaluate the software before committing to a full subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Check out securely using your preferred payment method — PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, or Google Pay are all accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your software">
    After a successful payment, you're redirected to your account dashboard. Your download link and license key are available immediately. A confirmation email with the same links is sent within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate and use">
    Download the software, enter your license key, and start using NinjaHackz.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The download link in your confirmation email is temporary and expires after 3 days. After that, always use your account dashboard to access your download.
</Note>
