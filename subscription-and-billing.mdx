NinjaHackz offers subscription plans that give you ongoing access to hacking challenge software. Your subscription is billed on a recurring cycle, and you can manage every aspect of it — including viewing invoices and cancelling — directly from your account dashboard.

## Subscription plans

NinjaHackz subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis. After each billing cycle, your subscription renews automatically so your access to the software continues without interruption.

<Tip>
  If you want to try the software before committing to a full subscription, a trial option is available for a small fee. It's the best way to evaluate NinjaHackz before purchasing a recurring plan.
</Tip>

## How billing works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select a subscription plan or the trial option on the NinjaHackz website. Pricing and plan details are shown at checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Pay securely at checkout. Your order is processed automatically — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your software immediately">
    After a successful payment, you're redirected to your account dashboard where your download link and license key are ready. A confirmation email with your download link is also sent within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic renewal">
    Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period. You'll receive an email notification before each renewal charge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The download link included in your confirmation email expires after 3 days. After that, log in to your account dashboard to access your download at any time.
</Note>

## Viewing your billing details

All billing information is available in your account dashboard on the NinjaHackz website. From there you can:

- View your current plan and next renewal date
- Review past invoices and payment history
- Update your payment method
- Cancel your subscription

## Trial option

A trial option is available for a small fee if you'd like to evaluate the software before purchasing a full subscription. The trial gives you access to NinjaHackz so you can decide whether a recurring plan is right for you.

## Refund policy

<Warning>
  NinjaHackz does not offer refunds on any purchases or subscriptions. Because software is delivered digitally and immediately upon payment, all sales are final. If you no longer want to be charged, cancel your subscription before the next billing date.
</Warning>

## Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation stops future billing — you retain access to the software until the end of your current billing period. See [Cancellation](/cancellation) for step-by-step instructions.
